About

Who we are:

Basically JYOT is a concept more than an organization…!

    Established on 5th September ’17, encouraged by the thought to uplift marginalized commonality and mitigate the darkness hindering its development by innovative ideas and thereby providing them a small JYOT- enough to give rays of hope to the lives.


Vision

  • Provide financial assistance and implement skill-based initiatives for all-round development of students.
  • Create a mutually strong generation by overcoming depression.
  • Foster the bond of affection between educated youth and underprivileged.
  • Integrate traditions and culture with novel ideas for social upliftment.





Mission

  • Reaching underprivileged to aid is our aim.

  • "Problem once faced, shouldn't be faced twice!" To accomplish this, our prime focus is providing indigents the guidance from experienced people who have already invested in such commonly encountered issues and have managed to find a way out..!

  • By working on wreck 'JYOT' family owns the strength to create eternal solutions for these objectives.

    Work

    What we do:

    An Eternal Treasure

      Old Age is another childhood! But the only difference is that everyone loves children but not their old parents. We decided to bring happiness back to their lives. With our visit to old age home, we discovered every wrinkle on their face has a different story to tell. Some had emotional stories, they had fought with every odd in front of them. We were able to add a new spirit of hope to their lives with our mere presence. Because “Nobody needs a smile so much as those who have none left to give”.

    Study Material Distribution: A Gift That Never Expires!

      Everyone, especially those with some financial problem struggles for notes and study materials in the first year of their college. So JYOT started one innovative initiative called the "Study Material distribution program”. In this program, notes were collected from 2nd, 3rd-year students and distributed to needy students of 1st year. To ensure that the notes reach actual needy and talented students data collection and face-to-face interviews were also done. Every year we are getting a huge response and hope we can continue to enlighten the lives of many.

    Shiv Jayanti: An Unending Era of Magnificence

      JYOT always comes up with unique ideas to achieve its objectives. To raise funds for the celebration of Shiv Jayanti, JYOT organized an aptitude test with nominal fees of 10 rupees. We again came up with an amazing idea of a "Chaha-Poha" stall. We raised funds without just asking for it. With these funds, 1 month of ration and solar lamps were given to children of Sadashanti Ashram Gruh.

    Planting Hope

      It is said “He who plants a tree. Plants a hope.” JYOT believes in the need for Tree plantation in the 21st century and to make it possible for the coming generations to see the mother earth at its best we have been coming forward with various tree plantation drives every year. We aim to plant more and more trees to ensure green Earth.

    "The true meaning of life is to plant trees, under whose shade you do not expect to sit."

    COVID Free Maharashtra

      Indulging our efforts for making betterment in the current scenario, members of JYOT stepped onto the grounds to make our society free from the sufferings of pandemic.
    Keeping in track, the information about all aspects of growth in no of Covid cases , increasing fatality rate &checking it’s spread through the carriers has been the action plan mapped by the entire team.
      Taking into consideration about all issues Jyot came up with an initiative of “COVID free Maharashtra" The first step included door to door survey , creating an awareness in the rural areas about disease, precautionary measures in the monotonous daily life schedules andself health care routines while in home quarantine, vaccination etc .... Surveys have been carried by them recently in Dhamangaon and Akot where they visited the rural areas and collected data regarding the same.
    Not only collection of data but also revisiting the homes after a span of 7 days to maintain a record about the increase in severity of the symptoms and suggesting them to get themselves hospitalized in nearby health care center to avoid further complications, is also a part of this survey. Team JYOT has been taking all the precautions while carrying on the survey. We have just stepped into the grounds, victory is yet to be achieved.

    Fiber of Ambition

      Indeed we all have our old and unused clothes piled up which we don’t want to wear anymore because they don’t fit or we don’t find them interesting at all and we often end up burning them & using them for cleaning chores. Underprivileged class of people can’t even afford pair of clothes to wear, having an option for that would be a part of luxury for them!
      So, for adding warmth and happiness in their lives, JYOT came up with an initiative of CLOTH DONATION. Members collected used and old clothes from the college students and donated them to little kids of Melghat region. Seeing their smiling faces after receiving that clothes, our hearts had an essence of realization about the irony our society is living with! .... Privileged kids fuss about getting short of the clothes that doesn’t fit for a particular list of occasions whilst some unfortunate children don't have one!
    What we realized after doing this act was, it felt as if we made those beautiful souls a part of our lives by making them fit into that pieces of clothes, we lived into someday!
    Having such experience can make you count the graciousness GOD have gifted you with! Feeling gratitude for what we have and sharing that with the ones who are deprived of the same , can add on that sheer beauty to your life you can’t express in words!
    Team JYOT feels grateful to have that feeling!

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • 1.What is the vision of JYOT?

      Answer: Our Vision is to bridge the gap between the educated youth and the underprivileged. We are on a mission to bring about big changes through our small but powerful initiatives. We are up to giving a better Tomorrow starting Today. If our efforts can make little positive change in society then they are justified!

    • 2. Who can join JYOT?

      Answer: Anybody who believes in our vision and has the same perspective to help others in need is more than welcome to be a part of Jyot. One can volunteer in our drives and contribute whatever one wishes through the organization.

    • 3. How can I join JYOT?

      Answer: Please go to our "Contact Us" section and contact our team. Also you can below founders and team members, you are always welcome to contribute with JYOT.

    • 4.What are various Initiatives that JYOT is involved with?

      Answer: We have been actively working on new and creative social drives with every year's blooming team of JYOT. We have successfully completed notes and study material donation drive, clothes distribution, educating children at the orphanage, etc. You can go on through the work section and history page to know more.

    • 5.How can I help other than making a financial contribution? What all can I give/contribute?

      Answer: It is not mandatory for anyone at JYOT to contribute financially. You can also give your services to society through JYOT by teaching children at the orphanage, donating old notes or clothes, helping out in small chores during various events organized by us. Also, your time and efforts are always welcomed.
      But if you want to contribute financially then you can go to Donate section where you can donate as TOGETHER WE CAN IMPROVE THEIR LIVES!

    • 6. Who runs the organization?

      Answer: JYOT depends upon its various teams consisting of various students from different paths of life. These students overall as an organization run JYOT.
